Welcome to the very first episode of "Stop this, podcast!" - the show where we talk about things that need to stop, and this is definitely one of them.

## What This Show Is About

This podcast is dedicated to identifying and discussing things that simply need to stop. Whether it's:

- Annoying social media trends
- Bad workplace practices  
- Harmful habits we've all picked up
- Things that just don't make sense anymore

We're here to call them out and hopefully inspire some positive change.

## Why Start This Show?

Honestly? Because there are too many things that need to stop, and not enough people talking about stopping them. We're all just going along with things that clearly don't work, don't make sense, or actively make our lives worse.

## What's Coming Up

In future episodes, we'll cover topics like:
- Stop checking your phone first thing in the morning
- Stop saying "sorry for the late reply" when it's been 2 hours
- Stop making meetings that could have been emails
- Stop using "unprecedented times" in every communication

## Get Involved

Have something you think needs to stop? We want to hear about it! Send us your suggestions and we might feature them in a future episode.

Thanks for listening, and remember: some things just need to stop, and that's okay!
